Who We Are

A hospital built for this community.

Cedar Medical Center opened its doors in 2004 with a clear purpose: to give our neighbourhood access to the kind of expert, caring medical treatment that was previously only available in distant city centres.

Today our 120-bed facility houses specialists across eight clinical departments, a 24-hour emergency unit, and more than 200 healthcare professionals who treat every patient like family.
