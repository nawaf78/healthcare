Our Departments

Everything you need, all in one place.

We believe fragmented care leads to worse outcomes. Every department at Cedar is designed to collaborate — your cardiologist speaks directly with your GP, your surgeon coordinates with your anaesthetist, and your care team shares a single record.

Emergency Medicine

24-hour emergency care for trauma, cardiac events, respiratory distress, and all life-threatening conditions. Average physician contact time: under 4 minutes.

24/7 Open Trauma Care Resuscitation Unit

Cardiology

Comprehensive cardiac diagnostics and treatment — from echocardiography and Holter monitoring to interventional procedures and post-cardiac rehabilitation programmes.

Echocardiography Intervention Cardiac Rehab

Paediatrics

Child-centred care from birth through adolescence. Our paediatric ward features dedicated play areas, family accommodation, and specialists trained exclusively in childhood medicine.

Neonatal Care Child Development Immunisation

Orthopaedics & Trauma

From sports injuries and fractures to joint replacement surgeries and spinal care — our orthopaedic team restores movement and quality of life with precision and care.

Joint Replacement Sports Injuries Spinal Care

Neurology

Diagnostic and treatment services for neurological conditions including stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, migraine, and multiple sclerosis — delivered by fellowship-trained neurologists.

Stroke Unit Epilepsy Care EEG & MRI

Oncology & Cancer Care

A full-spectrum oncology programme including medical oncology, chemotherapy, palliative care, and survivorship planning — with a multidisciplinary tumour board reviewing every complex case.

Chemotherapy Palliative Care Survivorship
How Your Care Works

Your Patient Journey

From your first call to your follow-up appointment — here is exactly what to expect when you come to Cedar Medical Center.

01

Book

Call us or book online in under two minutes. Our scheduling team matches you to the right specialist for your needs.

02

Consult

Meet your specialist in a calm, unhurried appointment. We allocate enough time to truly understand your situation.

03

Treat

Your treatment plan — whether medication, procedure, or surgery — is carried out by experienced specialists using modern facilities.

04

Recover

Structured follow-up care, physiotherapy, and long-term monitoring ensure your recovery is complete — not just started.
