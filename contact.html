Patient Questions

Before You Visit

For most specialist consultations, a referral from your GP is recommended but not always required. Emergency presentations and some outpatient clinics accept self-referrals. Contact our scheduling team and they will advise you on the right pathway for your situation.

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card (if applicable), a list of any current medications, and any relevant previous medical records or test results. Arriving 15 minutes before your appointment time is appreciated.

Yes. We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare and Medicaid. For a full list of accepted providers, or to verify your coverage before your visit, please contact our billing team on +1 (217) 555-0191.

In most wards, one family member may stay overnight with an admitted patient. Dedicated family accommodation is available in our Paediatrics unit for parents of child patients. ICU and post-surgery wards have restricted access — the nursing team will advise on a case-by-case basis.

Medical records requests can be submitted in writing to our Patient Relations team. Records are typically processed within 5–7 business days. You can also authorise another healthcare provider to request records directly on your behalf.
